About me

Hi there! i’m Nelson. A passionate frontend developer from Nigeria who loves experimenting, learning and building things with code. I’m somewhere in between an introvert and an extrovert, an ambivert there that’s the word. ;-)

I wanted to go into software engineering after watching an advert on TV that transcribes to : “Do you see an app or an entrepreneur shaping the future”. That ...

was how it started for me. I had my “aha” moment during an internship we were tasked to build a currency converter, it was challenging but after making some research i and my team was able to complete the task. I am currently learning how modern frontend works , It’s a constantly evolving field so i have to keep up.
